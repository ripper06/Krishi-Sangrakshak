Who Are We?

संरक्षण कृषि (Conservation tillage), खेती का एक बिल्कुल ही नया मॉडल है, जिसकी मदद से पर्यावरण का ख्याल रखा जाता है। इस अनोखी तरह की खेती में जमीन को या तो बिल्कुल भी नहीं जोता जाता (जुताई रहित कृषि) या फिर कम से कम जुताई होती है। इसमें फसल के पौधों को शुरू में नर्सरी में उगाया जाता है। साथ ही, लेजर की मदद से जमीन को जबरदस्त तरीके से समतल किया जाता है। ऊपर से, इसमें ड्रिप और स्प्रिंकलर्स जैसे सिंचाई के आधुनिक तरीकों का इस्तेमाल होता है। इन तरीकों का इस्तेमाल करके खेती में काफी मुनाफे का सौदा बन सकती है। साथ ही, इससे कृषि में इस्तेमाल होने वाली चीजों की कार्यकुशलता में जबरदस्त इजाफा होता है। इससे लागत कम आती है और पैदावार में काफी बढ़ोतरी होती है। इस सबका फायदा किसानों को मोटे मुनाफे के रूप में होता है। गंगा के मैदानों के किसानों के बीच खेती का यह नया तरीका काफी मशहूर हो रहा है। इसके पीछे बड़ी वजह है, इसका काफी अच्छा और फायदेमंद होना।

Our services

Prediction

Our website is dedicated to delivering advanced predictions in the fields of crop production, weather patterns, and yield forecasts. Leveraging cutting-edge technology and data analysis, we strive to provide users with timely and accurate information to enhance decision-making processes in agriculture. By offering early predictions for crop development, weather conditions, and yield estimates, farmers and agricultural stakeholders can proactively plan and optimize their operations. This proactive approach enables users to make informed choices regarding planting, harvesting, and resource management, ultimately contributing to increased efficiency and improved outcomes in the agricultural sector. Our commitment is to empower individuals within the agricultural community with the insights they need to navigate the complexities of farming in an ever-changing environment.

Helping hand of farmers!

Our website plays a vital role in preventing farmers' suicides by alleviating their financial burden through early predictions in crop production and yield forecasts. By providing accurate insights into expected crop performance and potential challenges, farmers can make informed decisions about resource allocation, minimizing the risk of crop failures and financial losses. Additionally, the ability to anticipate market conditions and optimize planting schedules enables farmers to strategize and maximize their yields. This proactive approach supports financial stability, reducing the economic stress that often contributes to the alarming rates of farmer suicides. Access to reliable information empowers farmers to adopt more sustainable and resilient farming practices, fostering long-term financial viability and mental well-being. Ultimately, our platform aims to be a valuable tool in promoting the economic sustainability of farmers and addressing the systemic issues that contribute to their financial struggles. Read More

Combatting Climate Change

Our website plays a crucial role in addressing and mitigating the impacts of climate change by providing early predictions for crop performance, weather patterns, and yield forecasts. With this information, farmers can optimize resource allocation, adjust planting schedules, and implement sustainable practices to adapt to changing climate conditions. By facilitating more precise and efficient agricultural practices, our platform contributes to the reduction of greenhouse gas emissions associated with traditional farming methods. Additionally, improved yield forecasts aid in minimizing food waste and enhancing overall agricultural productivity, which can reduce the need for deforestation and land conversion. Through the empowerment of informed decision-making in agriculture, our website serves as a valuable tool in the collective effort to combat climate change and promote a more sustainable future for our planet .
